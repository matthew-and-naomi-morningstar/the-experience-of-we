# Threshold 6: Transforming

*Embodying new patterns until they become natural.*

There is a difference between knowing a practice and being changed by it. Reading about deep listening doesn't make us listen deeply. Practicing it once in a moment of clarity doesn't either. Transformation is what happens when a new way of being slowly becomes the default — when, under stress, the pattern that emerges is the one we have been training rather than the one we inherited.

This threshold is for that slower process. Repatterning. Integrating new ways of being even when activation tries to pull us back to old ones. Recognizing regression early, before it becomes a full retreat. Completing the small cycles of practice so they actually compound. Developing the kind of toughness that does not require hardness — the capacity to stay with what is hard without abandoning who we are becoming.
