# Connect and Contribute

*An Invitation to Participate in a Living System*

---

Hello, dear companions.

The Experience of We is unfinished by design. It stays alive the way any living thing does: by taking in what's real, metabolizing it, and changing as it goes. That means it doesn't only grow when we write more of it. It grows when *you* take it into your own life and bring something back, what worked, what didn't, what was missing, what you made of it that we never imagined.

So your participation isn't a bonus or an afterthought. It's part of how this work stays alive. We would genuinely love to hear from you.

~Matthew and Naomi Danielle Morningstar

---

## Ways to take part

**Practice it.** The whole point is to take this into real relationships, yours. You don't need our permission, a credential, or a finished understanding. Begin where you are, with whoever is beside you.

**Adapt it.** This work is released under a Creative Commons Attribution-ShareAlike license (CC BY-SA 4.0). You're free to share it, adapt it, translate it, and build on it, for your relationship, your family, your community, as long as you credit the source and share what you build under the same terms. If you make something good with it, that belongs to everyone too. And we'd rather you adapt it thoughtfully than preserve it perfectly: living things survive by responding to the realities they actually inhabit, and the same is true of this work. If your life asks for something different from what we wrote, trust that.

**Tell us what you find.** This matters more than you might think. The framework names lived experience as one of its ways of knowing, and yours is one we don't have. Tell us what landed and what didn't, where a practice helped and where it fell flat, where something felt off, where we got it wrong. Disagreement especially: the work is meant to be kept honest, and you can't keep something honest if you only ever hear from people who agree.

There's a deeper way to see this, too. Every relationship that genuinely lives these practices becomes another place where this work meets reality, another test of whether it's true. So when you tell us what you found, you aren't reporting to us. You're helping reality teach the system. That's how a living thing learns.

**Help build it.** If you want to contribute more directly, to translate, to facilitate, to bring your own expertise to the work, reach out and tell us what you're drawn to. This was never meant to be built by two people alone.

---

## How to reach us

Write to us at **matthewandnaomi@experienceofwe.com**. We're two people, not an institution, so we can't promise a fast reply. But we read what comes, and we're glad when it does.

For more about the work, you can visit **experienceofwe.com**.

One honest note, offered with care: we're not a crisis service, and we can't provide real-time or emergency support. If you're in acute distress, please reach for professional help or a local crisis line. The work will still be here when the ground beneath you is steadier. (Our *Before You Begin* guide says more about this, gently.)

---

## A closing word

This work is our response to a world that has forgotten how much we belong to each other. We're putting it out freely because it was never ours to hoard. It's a living thing, and living things are meant to be shared, used, changed, and grown.

You're already part of it, simply by being here and considering it. If something in this resonates, we'd love to know. And if you carry it somewhere we never could, that isn't a departure from the work. That is the work, doing exactly what it's for.

With real gladness that you're here,

~Matthew and Naomi Danielle Morningstar
