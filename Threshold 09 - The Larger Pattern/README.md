# Threshold 9: The Larger Pattern

*Meta-practices that synthesize the whole journey.*

At a certain point in the practice, what we are doing in any single moment begins to participate in a larger pattern we can learn to perceive. The Awareness Spiral names how attention itself moves through the field over time. The Catalyst Spiral names how transformation happens — not as event but as cyclic process. Fluid Awareness is the sustained capacity to move with the flow of experience rather than gripping at it. Exploring Spirituality is the practice of relating, together, to what is larger than us.

These are not advanced techniques layered on top of the earlier practices. They are the larger patterns the earlier practices have been participating in all along. This threshold is for becoming conscious of those patterns and learning to engage them deliberately.

Four practices.

1. The Awareness Spiral Together
2. The Catalyst Spiral Together
3. Developing Fluid Awareness Together
4. Exploring Spirituality Together
