# About The Experience of We

*What this work is, why it's named this, and how it comes to know what it knows.*

These three documents are the foundation the rest of the repository rests on. They don't teach a practice; they establish how to hold the practices responsibly. You don't need them to begin, but they're worth reading before, or alongside, the work.

3. [**Our Ways of Knowing**](03%20Our%20Ways%20of%20Knowing%20-%20The%20Ways%20We%20Come%20to%20Know%2C%20and%20How%20We%20Hold%20Them.md) — How a living system decides what's true: many fallible ways of knowing, held under humility.
