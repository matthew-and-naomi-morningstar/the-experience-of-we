# Orientation

*Documents that help you enter The Experience of We.*

The suggested sequence below moves from invitation through honest assessment to shared commitment to map to practical how-to. A parallel way in opens at the end.

## Suggested sequence

*Entry, assessment, shared commitment*

1. [**Welcome to The Experience of We**](./01%20Welcome%20to%20The%20Experience%20of%20We%20-%20An%20Invitation%20to%20the%20Journey%20of%20Reunion.md) — What this is. Who finds their way here. The first honest orientation.
2. [**Before You Begin**](./02%20Before%20You%20Begin%20-%20What%20This%20Work%20Asks%20and%20When%20to%20Wait.md) — What the work asks of you and the relationship. When to wait. The threshold guardian.
3. [**The Covenant of Entry**](./03%20The%20Covenant%20of%20Entry%20into%20The%20Experience%20of%20We.md) — The formal shared commitment. Seven foundational agreements. Read together, spoken aloud if possible.

*The map and the ideas behind it*

4. [**The Relational Arc**](./04%20The%20Relational%20Arc%20-%20A%20Guide%20to%20the%20Journey%20of%20We%20Practices.md) — The map. Ten thresholds, the developmental sequence, the spiral structure.
5. [**The Concept Map**](./05%20The%20Concept%20Map%20-%20A%20Guide%20to%20the%20Ideas%20Behind%20We%20Practices.md) — The ideas behind the practices, gathered in one place for reference.

*How to actually practice*

6. [**Taking On Practices Together**](./06%20Taking%20On%20Practices%20Together%20-%20A%20Shared%20Guide%20to%20How%20We%20Build%20Relational%20Skills.md) — How to actually use the practice guides: reading, discussing, practicing, returning.

*Situational, if relevant to your circumstances*

7. [**Inviting a Companion**](./07%20Inviting%20a%20Companion%20-%20A%20Guide%20to%20Sharing%20This%20Practice%20With%20Someone%20You%20Trust.md) — How to extend the invitation without pressure.
8. [**Beginning on Your Own**](./08%20Beginning%20on%20Your%20Own%20-%20A%20Guide%20to%20Practicing%20Before%20You%20Find%20Your%20Companion.md) — How to practice alone until a partner is available.

## A parallel way in

[**Understanding Relational Micro-Practices**](./Understanding%20Relational%20Micro-Practices%20-%20A%20Shared%20Guide%20to%20What%20They%20Are%20and%20How%20They%20Stack.md) — Small, repeatable practices that can be picked up in single moments: a breath, a sentence, a pause. Parallel to *The Relational Arc*: a different way in, for relationships that want to begin with something small.
