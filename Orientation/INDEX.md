# Orientation

*Documents that help you enter The Experience of We.*

These are read before any practice begins. They move from invitation through honest assessment to shared commitment to map to practical how-to.

## Suggested sequence

1. **Welcome to The Experience of We** — What this is. Who finds their way here. The first honest orientation.
2. **Before You Begin** — What the work asks of you and the relationship. When to wait. The threshold guardian.
3. **The Covenant of Entry** — The formal shared commitment. Seven foundational agreements. Read together, spoken aloud if possible.
4. **The Relational Arc** — The map. Ten thresholds, the developmental sequence, the spiral structure.
5. **The Concept Map** — The ideas behind the practices, gathered in one place for reference.
6. **Taking On Practices Together** — How to actually use the practice guides: reading, discussing, practicing, returning.
7. **Inviting a Companion** — How to extend the invitation without pressure.
8. **Beginning on Your Own** — How to practice alone until a partner is available.

*8 documents total*
